Yu-Gi-Oh: Intermediate Algebra Problems

21. In Yu-Gi-Oh!, your monster has an attack power of x. After an increase of 8, its attack power becomes 20. What was the original attack power?

22. You're summoning a monster in Yu-Gi-Oh! The monster's health is x after it gets a 10-point boost, making the total health 30. What was the original health?

23. Your monster starts with x defense points and loses 5 points. Now it has 10 defense points left. What was the original defense?

24. A spell card boosts your monster's attack by 7, giving it x points. After the boost, the total attack points are 22. How many points did your monster have originally?

25. You use a trap card that takes away 9 points from your opponent's monster, leaving it with x attack points. Now it has 11 attack points. How many did it have originally?

26. A monster starts with x health and loses 6 points in battle, now it has 14 health left. What was its original health?

27. A spell card adds x points to your defense. You now have 17 defense points after a 7-point boost. How many did you start with?

28. You start with x magic points and lose 8 in battle, now you have 10 points left. What was your starting amount?

29. A monster's attack is boosted by x points, and now it has 21 points. If the boost was 5 points, what was the original attack?

30. Your monster has x defense points and takes 7 damage, leaving it with 12 defense. How many points did it start with?

31. You summon a monster with x attack points. After gaining 4 more points, it now has 16. What was the original attack?

32. Your opponent's monster loses x defense points after an attack. Now it has 13 points left, but originally had 18. How many points did it lose?

33. A trap card reduces your opponent's attack by 6 points. Now it has x points left, and had 15 before. How many does it have left?

34. You summon a monster with x defense points. It gains 8 points from a spell card, and now has 24 defense. How many points did it have initially?

35. Your monster loses 7 attack points, leaving it with 14 attack points. How many did it have before losing the points?

36. You activate a spell that adds x attack points to your monster. After the boost, it now has 23 points. If the spell gave 7 points, how many did it originally have?

37. Your monster loses 6 defense points during a battle, leaving it with 8 points. How many did it start with?

38. You summon a monster with x attack points, and it gains 5 more. Now it has 25 attack points. How many did it originally have?

39. Your opponent's monster loses 4 defense points, leaving it with 12 defense points. How many points did it originally have?

40. You have x health points and gain 6 more. Now you have 18 points. How many did you have originally?
