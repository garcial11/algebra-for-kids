Croatia Soccer: Intermediate Algebra Problems

41. You're playing for Croatia. You scored x goals. After 2 more goals, the total score is 7. How many goals did you score initially?

42. In a match, your team has x players on the field, and 2 more substitutes come in. Now there are 11 players. How many were there at first?

43. You scored x goals, but 3 were disallowed. Now you have 5 goals. How many did you score originally?

44. You started with x goals, then scored 5 more, making a total of 9. How many goals did you start with?

45. Croatia wins by scoring x goals. After losing 2 goals due to offside, they now have 3 goals. How many did they originally score?

46. You saved x penalty shots. After making 4 more saves, your total saves are 9. How many saves did you make at first?

47. Your team concedes x goals, and after losing 2 players, the defense stops 8 goals. How many goals did the team concede initially?

48. Croatia starts with x players on the field, but 1 player is injured, leaving 10 players. How many were there at the start?

49. The team scores x goals, and then scores 4 more, making a total of 12 goals. How many goals did they score initially?

50. You win a soccer match by scoring x goals, but lose 2 due to fouls, leaving you with 6 goals. How many goals did you score originally?
